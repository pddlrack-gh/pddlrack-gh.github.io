A modern pickleball app for building connections, not finding courts.



Say goodbye to mismatched open play.

Lopsided games aren't fun for anyone. PaddleRack brings transparency to the courts, connecting you with local players who match your skill level using our community-driven rating system.



One app to replace all your group text chaos.

Ditch the messy text threads and chaotic GroupMe chats. PaddleRack lets you create sessions, easily manage and track who's coming, and lock in a full group of similarly skilled players with just a few taps. All in one place.

Your weekly games, on autopilot.

No more weekly "Are we on?" texts needed with PaddleRack's recurring sessions. Just set your refresh schedule, who you want to invite, and we'll handle the invitations and RSVP tracking for you automatically.

Traveling? No local connections? No problem.

PaddleRack bridges the gap when you're away from home or looking to expand your network. Join pre-existing local sessions or host your own to attract trusted, peer-rated players in any zip code.
